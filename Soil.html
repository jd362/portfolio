Soil VR Project

Soil VR Project is the second project I participated in the WID Virtual Environment Group. It is a virtual education tour of the microscopic soil world enabled by VR headset.

We modeled the scanned soil sample provided by soil science lab and presented it in Unity. The game will shrink the player to the size of microscopic soil particles. Along a designed track, it takes the player to travel through particles, and educate them the underlying mystery of soil.
