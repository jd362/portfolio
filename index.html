Hello!

I'm Jack Dong. I'm currently pursuing my Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University.

I have a double major undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Psychology from University of Wisconsin-Madison. My study in two different subjects equipped me with solid technical skills and unique psychological insight, laying a strong foundation for the pursuit of my passion- game design and development.

I worked as a research assistant in the Wisconsin Institute for Discovery and participated in multiple virtual reality game projects. From that I accumulated rich experience in Unity development and VR user experience designing.

My interest in games extended to Artificial Intelligence, as I realized that highly intelligent AI could be the rule changer for game experience in the future. So, I chose to pursue further education in AI Engineering at CMU. I'm working as a student research assistant, and my team is exploring techniques to improve AI-human cooperation in games.

This portfolio includes demonstration of my past project experiences. Hope you enjoy it. GLHF!
